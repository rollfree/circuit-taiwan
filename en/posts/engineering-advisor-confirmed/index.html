Engineering Advisor Confirmed: Technical Architecture Locked

Bernard joins Circuit Taiwan as Engineering Advisor, bringing route experience and safety-system expertise.

Circuit Taiwan is pleased to confirm that Bernard has joined the project as Engineering Advisor.

A skilled engineer with extensive Taiwan route experience - including multiple completed circuits of the island - and native French fluency, Bernard brings three critical layers to the expedition:

  1. Technical Architecture: Validation of the integrated weather system, solar charging integration, and offline-capable safety protocols.
  2. Route Intelligence: First-hand knowledge of Su-Hua conditions, east-coast extraction points, and typhoon-response logistics.
  3. Cultural Authenticity: Native French speaker embedded in the core team = genuine content layer for the French-speaking media partnerships.

What This Means for the Expedition#

AreaImpact
Safety SystemIndependent engineering review of alert logic, sensor fusion, and false-alarm mitigation. Field-testing protocol refined before departure.
Vehicle StrategyTechnical input on two-vehicle handover, solar charging integration, and energy-management logging.
French ContentAuthentic French voice in planning, filming, and post-production; a unique lane for Arte, RTBF, and WBI partnerships.
Sponsor CredibilityEngineering validation adds weight to technical sponsorship conversations (Delta, AUO, Advantech, CMC).

Potential Driver / Vehicle Assistance Role#

Depending on travel economics and sponsorship confirmation, Bernard may also serve as Assistance-Vehicle Driver for all or part of the expedition.

Why This Is Contingent#

FactorCurrent Reality
Airfare VolatilityTranscontinental flights from Europe to Taiwan remain elevated due to global fuel-market dynamics.
Sponsorship TimingVehicle and technical sponsor commitments determine budget flexibility for international team travel.
Local BackupA qualified local driver remains the reliable baseline; Bernard’s participation is an enhancement, not a dependency.

If Activated, Bernard Brings#

  • ✅ Route Mastery: Has ridden the east coast and Su-Hua multiple times; knows shelter locations, signal dead zones, and extraction protocols.
  • ✅ Technical Oversight: Can operate the vehicle while monitoring safety-system telemetry and energy management.
  • ✅ Bilingual Communication: Native French + fluent English + Taiwan experience = smoother coordination with French media and sponsors.
  • ✅ Team Cohesion: Pre-existing trust with the expedition leader = clearer decision-making under pressure.

The expedition does not depend on any single individual. It depends on clear roles, verified systems, and adaptable planning. Bernard’s engineering role is confirmed. His driver role is a flexible option that strengthens the plan if travel economics align.

Next Steps#

  • Bernard begins remote advisory work immediately: safety-system review, route intelligence documentation, and French-content workflow planning.
  • Travel decision will be made 6 months before departure, based on:
    • Confirmed sponsorship budget
    • Airfare trends
    • Local driver availability
  • All updates will be published on this platform.
