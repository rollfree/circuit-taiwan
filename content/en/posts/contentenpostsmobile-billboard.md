## Traditional Mobile Billboards vs. Circuit Taiwan

| Feature | City Ad Vehicle | Circuit Taiwan Assistance Vehicle |
|---------|----------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Coverage** | One city, repetitive loops | Entire Taiwan coastal circuit (~1,200 km) |
| **Duration** | Daily 8-hour shifts | 35 consecutive days, dawn to dusk |
| **Context** | Urban streets only | Coastal highways, mountain passes, indigenous villages, urban centers |
| **Content Integration** | Static ads, no storytelling | Filmed daily; appears in documentary footage, social media, research logs |
| **Iconic Locations** | Traffic intersections | Su-Hua Highway cliffs, Taitung 7-Eleven, Tamsui finish line, sunrise at Kenting |
| **Audience Reach** | Local commuters | Global viewers via trilingual content + investor reports |

## Why This Matters for Sponsors

### 🎥 Always On Camera
The assistance vehicle is never parked in a garage. It is:
- Always moving or visibly stopped at a named location
- Filmed every day for documentary and social content
- Present in every piece of media produced during the expedition

### 🌍 Unmatched Geographic Range
No city advertising vehicle gets:
- That variety of scenic and cultural context
- That quality of associated storytelling content
- That geographic range across Taiwan's diverse landscapes

### 🔗 Integrated Brand Exposure
Your logo doesn't just drive around — it:
- Appears alongside medical telemetry data collection
- Is featured in accessibility mapping fieldwork
- Shows up in cultural documentation with indigenous communities
- Becomes part of a narrative about innovation, resilience, and adaptation

## Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Exposure | Deliverables |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Primary** | Full-wrap vehicle branding | Logo in all video content, dedicated social posts, final report feature |
| **Secondary** | Side-panel branding | Logo in select content, mention in research outputs |
| **Supporting** | Rear-panel or equipment branding | Logo on project website, acknowledgment in documentation |

*All partnerships include high-resolution photo/video assets for sponsor reuse.*
