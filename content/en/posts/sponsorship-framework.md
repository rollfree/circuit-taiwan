Sponsors receive a defined contractual return: publication-quality content, field performance data, and documented accessibility verification. Not vague exposure. The expedition leader has over 1,000 published articles and 40 years in Taiwan. Content delivery is proven, not promised.

## Three Currencies of Value

| Currency | Description | Benefits Most |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Financial Value** | Hardware, services, and cash equivalent. Comparable across all sponsors. Determines baseline tier. | All sponsors: the foundation metric |
| **Visual Presence** | How many frames does the product appear in over 35 days? The wheelchair and helmet are in every frame. The smart glasses are in every first-person shot. | KARMA Medical, Giant, Atunas, smartwatch/glasses sponsors |
| **Data Value** | Engineering and medical data with genuine commercial R&D value. More valuable to some sponsors than any amount of social media reach. | Delta, AUO, Dynavolt, medical partner, CMC |

Different currency, equal dignity. Every sponsorship conversation begins with one question: what do YOU most value?

## Sponsorship Tiers

| Tier | Threshold | Return |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Lead Partner** (1 slot) | Highest total contribution | Lead position on all materials including vehicle livery. Press conference. Full engineering and medical dataset. Named in all publications and film credits. Exclusive story rights in category for 12 months post-expedition. |
| **Major Partner** (up to 6 slots) | NT$200,000–500,000 equivalent | Category exclusivity. Official Category Partner status. Strong branding on relevant equipment and vehicle sideboards. Dedicated field performance report. Minimum 4 dedicated articles. Named in documentary. |
| **Category Partner** (open) | NT$50,000–200,000 equivalent | Category exclusivity during expedition. Logo on relevant equipment. Vehicle sideboard option. Product feature in content. Named in publications. |
| **Technical / Community** | Hardware/services < NT$50,000 or any contribution | Logo. Product feature in engineering log. Web and social mentions. Website listing. Thank-you content. |

## Category Exclusivity: How Spending Unlocks Protection

Exclusivity is not automatic. It is earned through contribution level and formalised in writing.

| Tier | Category Exclusivity | What It Means |
|------|---------------------|---------------|
| **Lead Partner** | Full category exclusivity + first-right-of-refusal on adjacent categories | No other sponsor in your category for 12 months post-expedition. First option to extend partnership for Phase 2. |
| **Major Partner** | Category exclusivity during expedition + 6 months post | Your brand is the only one in your category visible on expedition materials for the duration of the project plus half a year. |
| **Category Partner** | Category exclusivity during expedition only | While the expedition is active, you are the sole representative of your product category. |
| **Technical / Community** | No exclusivity | Multiple brands may appear in the same category. Visibility is shared. |

### What "Category Exclusivity" Actually Means
- ✅ **Your logo** appears on relevant equipment, vehicle panels, or content
- ✅ **No competitor** in your defined category is accepted as a sponsor during the protected period
- ✅ **First-right-of-refusal** if a new, adjacent category emerges
- ❌ **Not a monopoly** on the entire expedition—other categories remain open
- ❌ **Not perpetual**—exclusivity expires per the tier schedule above

### Defining the Category
Exclusivity only works if the category is clearly defined *before* signing. Examples:
- **Giant** → Safety helmet, gloves, hi-vis gear
- **Atunas** → Thermoregulating apparel, rain protection
- **Delta** → Energy management system, charge controller
- **Louisa Coffee** → Breakfast and coffee service

The category definition is attached to every sponsorship agreement. Ambiguity is removed before money changes hands.

### The Exclusivity Trade-Off
Higher exclusivity = higher contribution threshold. This is transparent from the first conversation:

> *"If you want to be the only helmet brand on this expedition, the Major Partner tier is the entry point. If you are comfortable sharing visibility with one other brand, the Category Partner tier may suit you."*

No surprises. No renegotiation. Just clear, commercial terms.
